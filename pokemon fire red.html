When you enter a game, press the three lines in the top right corner, and then the game controller button in the bottom left. Then turn on virtual controls. If it doesn't work, turn off and then back on. When you are done playing, tap on the three lines again, and press the file shaped save button in the bottom left corner. Then press download. This will save your game. If you want to load your game, tap the three lines again, and press the button to the right of the save button. Press "choose file", then go to "my ipad", and press downloads. Your save will be there. Everytime you save, the number goes up. Tap on the one with the highest number for your most recent save. If the game isnt giving any menu try checking you are on the mobile website
